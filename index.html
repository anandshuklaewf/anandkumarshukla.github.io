About Me

I am Anand Kumar Shukla, Director of Ekvida Welfare Foundation. I come from a village that has been labeled as an industrial area for over 20 years. Despite the presence of 400+ companies, our small area's most basic needs remain unmet. Children are dropping out of school, getting addicted to harmful substances, young girls are leaving education early, diseases are increasing, and infrastructure like roads and drains is in poor shape.

I couldn’t ignore this. I had two options — fight with everyone or become capable enough to transform the system myself. I chose the second. I began a new journey with a vision to make Som Gram Sabha and nearby areas among India’s top 10 most growth-driven villages by 2035.

Mission

To build a secure, literate, aware, and empowered society by enlightening and enabling rural citizens in every possible way — through the work of Ekvida Welfare Foundation.

Vision

To transform Som Gram Sabha into one of the top 10 growth-oriented villages in India by the year 2035.
