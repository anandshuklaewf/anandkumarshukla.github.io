About Me

I’m Anand Kumar Shukla, a passionate youth leader dedicated to social development. I served as a volunteer at Mejlol Organization from 2021 to November 2023, where I learned public engagement and all the essential grassroots skills. Disheartened by the poor conditions in rural villages, I took a stand to bring meaningful change — and that’s when Ekvida Welfare Foundation was born. My core purpose is to ensure mental and physical growth of children and empower society through sustainable actions.
